Oompah Brass

The UK's Original Rock 'n' Roll Oompah Band

Music

Oompah Brass have performed on TV and Radio, and their tracks have been used on a range of BBC productions.

They have featured on BBC Radio 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6, and BBC London, and have appeared live on the shows of Danny Baker, Chris Evans, Richard Bacon, Stuart Maconie, Sean Keaveny to name just a few.
